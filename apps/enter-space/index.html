Seamless Apple Integration

Enter Space integrates directly with macOS Finder and iOS Files app through File Provider technology. Access all 62 cloud storage providers as if they were native drives on your device.

Drag and drop files, use Spotlight search, and work with your cloud files using any app—just like they're stored locally. Full read and write support for the majority of supported clouds.

Bank-Grade Security

Your files are encrypted on your device before they're uploaded to the cloud. Enter Space uses end-to-end encryption to ensure only you can access your data, even if the cloud provider is compromised.

Built on Rclone's proven open-source technology with years of community testing and security audits. Fast, reliable, and secure cloud access you can trust.
