⚡ Zap

Race to Answer. Press Your Luck.

Trivia meets blackjack. Win the trivia race, then press your luck on a grid of cards — keep flipping to grow your pot or lock it in, but draw a Zap and you bust. Duel friends over Game Center or right inside an iMessage. 21 categories, 5,000+ questions, delivered fresh over the air.

Why Zap Feels Different

Trivia With a Charge

Speed matters, every answer crackles, and your friends are one tap away

🃏

Press Your Luck

Answer correctly and you earn a draw from a shared 3×3 grid. Flip cards to bank 1, 2, or 3 points — keep going or lock your pot. But two Zaps are hiding, and one bust wipes the round.

🎯

21 Categories

General Knowledge, Science, History, Geography, Film & TV, Music, Sports, Video Games, Anime, Bible, and more — over 5,000 questions in all.

📲

iMessage Duels

Start a match and answer your turn right inside the conversation — one question per bubble. Send it, your friend answers, back and forth.

🏆

Game Center Matches

Live head-to-head and async turn-based duels with friends or random opponents, plus Most Wins, Solo High Score, Biggest Round, and Lucky Streak leaderboards and 12 achievements.

👥

Pass N' Play

One device, two players, a privacy handoff screen between turns. Perfect for the couch or a road trip.

🔄

Always Fresh

Questions update over the air from a curated, version-controlled source — new content lands without an App Store update.
