A stronghold by design

The whole point of Haven is that nobody holds the keys to your life — not a company, not an algorithm, not even the person who built it.

  • ✓
    Your keys never leave your phoneGenerated on-device and kept in the Secure Enclave. There's no account database to breach.
  • ✓
    No central serverPosts travel peer-to-peer, end-to-end encrypted. There's no warehouse of your data, anywhere.
  • ✓
    The maker can't read your stuffBy design there is no master key and no backdoor. Blaine can't be compelled to hand over what he never holds.
  • ✓
    No PII, everNo phone number or email to sign up. You're a key and a name you choose — nothing more.
  • ✓
    You're in controlBlock anyone instantly. On-device sensitive-content guards. You decide who's in, with friendly safety words to confirm it's really them.
  • ✓
    Open and auditableThe code is source-available on GitHub. Don't trust the claims — read them.
