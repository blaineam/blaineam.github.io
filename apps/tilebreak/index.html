🀄 Tilebreak

Match. Combo. Clear.

A polished modern Mahjong solitaire for iPhone and iPad. Tap two matching free tiles to break the pair, build a combo, and clear the board. Every deal is provably solvable. No ads. No tracking. Plays offline.

What's inside

Built for the long game

Seven layouts, six themes, four face sets, two rule variants — and not a single ad in sight

🧩

Always solvable

Every board is generated via reverse-removal — we simulate a valid play-through first, then deal the tiles into the resulting order. No more random shuffles that lock you out two minutes in.

🗺️

Seven layouts

Garden, Pyramid, Turtle, Cross, Diamond, Tower, and Bricks. Each one is 144 tiles tuned for portrait phones and reads beautifully on iPad.

🎨

Six themes, four face sets

Mix Ivory, Jade, Midnight, Cherry, Lantern, or Cyber with Classic Mahjong, Icons, Garden, or Animals tile faces. Live-switch any of it from Settings without dropping your game.

🎵

Procedural soundtrack

Each theme ships with its own non-repetitive procedural music engine driven by AVAudio's sample-accurate scheduler. Respects your silent switch.

🔥

Combo + time scoring

Fast clears multiply your score. Combo streaks stack up to 5×. A misaimed tap breaks the streak — so does using a hint, but only if you cash it in.

🏆

Game Center leaderboards

Three global leaderboards: best single-game score, fastest clear, and longest combo. End-of-game screen shows your personal record next to each stat and highlights a new best in gold.

📵

Offline, ad-free

No ads. No tracking. No analytics. No required sign-in. Plays everywhere, including airplane mode. Game Center features need a connection but the core game does not.

🤝

Challenge a friend

After a win, send a real native Game Center challenge tied to your actual leaderboard entry. They get an in-game banner with the score to beat.

🔋

Light on battery

SwiftUI + SpriteKit hybrid, 60 fps cap, zero third-party SDKs. Stack lift, side walls, and shadows are all proportional so the board scales cleanly from iPhone mini to iPad 13".

Pick a layout. Pick a style.

Tilebreak ships with seven board layouts — from the classic Garden, to a brick-stacked Pyramid, to a Latin Cross that fans out across the screen. Whichever you pick, the deal is provably solvable.

Combine any layout with one of six themes and four face sets. Want Classic Mahjong tiles on a midnight background with a lo-fi soundtrack? Done. Want full-color animal emoji on a synthwave Cyber theme? Also done.

A real challenge, not a slot machine.

Time and combo both matter. A fast clear with a long streak posts a serious score. A slow clear with broken combos posts a fine one. Used a hint? Took an undo? You'll see the deduction.

The end screen pulls your personal best from Game Center and shows it next to every stat. Beat your record and the row goes gold — there's no ambiguity about whether you crushed it.

Made with care, shipped with care.

Tilebreak is hand-built in SwiftUI and SpriteKit — no Unity, no third-party SDKs, no analytics, no ads. The classic Mahjong tile faces are drawn procedurally in CoreGraphics so they look crisp at any tile size.

The app icon is procedural too: two matching Red Dragon tiles flanking a burst of sparks, generated by a Swift script so it can be tweaked and re-rendered in seconds. The whole thing is iOS 26 native and built against Swift 6 strict concurrency.
